Social Insights Studio

Connect and visualize your TikTok performance data in Looker Studio.

What is Social Insights Studio?

Social Insights Studio is a secure, production-ready connector for Google Looker Studio that allows TikTok creators to visualize their performance data — including follower counts, video metrics, and audience engagement — in powerful dashboards.

Why Use It?

  • View profile metrics like followers and likes over time
  • Analyze video performance (views, shares, comments)
  • Connect directly via TikTok's secure Login Kit (OAuth2)

How It Works

  1. Authorize with TikTok via a secure login
  2. Your data is pulled securely from TikTok APIs
  3. Customize and build your own dashboards in Looker Studio
