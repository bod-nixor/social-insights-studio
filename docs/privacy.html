Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 08/06/2025

The Board of Directors ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Social Insights Studio Connector ("the Connector"). This policy explains how we handle data when you use our service.

1. Data Collection

  • Does NOT collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Retrieves TikTok performance data (e.g., metrics, video/user analytics) via TikTok’s Open Platform API only after your explicit authorization.
  • Processes data solely within Google Looker Studio; no data is retained by us.

2. Data Use

Purpose: To fetch and visualize TikTok analytics in Looker Studio based on your API permissions.
No Secondary Use: We do not modify, sell, or distribute your data.
Transparency: All data flows directly between TikTok’s API and your Looker Studio instance.

3. Third-Party Services

Your data is subject to the policies of TikTok and Google Looker Studio.

4. Data Security

  • OAuth 2.0 Authentication: All API requests are encrypted; we never store credentials.
  • No Servers Involved: Data passes through the Connector but is not logged or cached.

5. User Control

You may revoke access or disconnect the app via TikTok Developer settings.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Revisions will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
